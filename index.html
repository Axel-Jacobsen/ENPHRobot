The Problem

Four Ewoks and Chewbacca are trapped in the Empire Stronghold! Our task was to create an autonomous robot to navigate the stronghold and return the rescuees to the start of the course.

To do this, we had to navigate over gaps, identify and react to alternating IR frequencies, climb a ramp, and deploy a basket to return our captured critters.
